About

Quietly curious, stubbornly practical.

I grew up wanting to understand how things — from compilers to coffee — really work. That curiosity took me through computer science at ECNU's Software Engineering Institute, into research on intelligent systems, and into the everyday craft of writing code that other humans can read months later.

Off the keyboard you'll find me reading long-form essays, brewing single-origin coffee, tinkering with home networking, and writing the occasional short story. I believe a personal site should feel like a quiet living room — small, honest, and easy to leave.
